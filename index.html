Who Is Dedeh 254?

Dedeh 254 is a kenyan HipHop musician born and raised Kenya. He is a musical prodigy from East Africa. This Kenyan born Artist's career quickly gained momentum with the release of “#Stealing The Show,” a song off his critically acclaimed first album, to be released in 2023.
